Privacy Policy

Liquid Glass Player · Last updated June 29, 2026

Liquid Glass Player is built so that your data stays yours. We do not run a server that collects, stores, or sees your information. This policy explains what that means in plain terms.

The short version

  • We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data.
  • There are no accounts to create and no analytics or tracking in the app.
  • Your playlists and streaming content remain entirely on your own device — they never pass through a server we control.

How the app works

Liquid Glass Player allows you to paste M3U or M3U8 playlist URLs to stream video content directly on your iPhone or iPad. The app simply fetches the playlist and streams the media directly from the sources you provide using Apple's built-in playback stack. We never receive, record, or have access to any of your playlists or the content you watch.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect personal information, does not contain advertising or third-party trackers, and does not use analytics SDKs. All of your favorites and playlist data are saved locally on your device.

Device permissions

The app requests access to the internet to fetch and stream the playlist URLs you provide. It is not used to gather information about your network or other devices.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on our GitHub support page.
