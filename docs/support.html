Liquid Glass Player

Liquid Glass Player is a native SwiftUI IPTV player for iPhone and iPad. Here's how to get set up and where to get help.

Need help or found a bug?

Open an issue on our GitHub support page and we'll get back to you. Please include your device model, iOS version, and a description of your issue.

Getting started

  1. Copy an M3U or M3U8 playlist URL that you have the right to access.
  2. Open Liquid Glass Player on your iPhone or iPad.
  3. Paste the URL into the app to add your playlist.
  4. Browse channels, search categories, or save favorites to start watching.

Frequently asked questions

Where do I get a playlist?

Liquid Glass Player is a media player and does not provide any content, channels, or playlists. You are responsible for providing your own M3U/M3U8 playlists containing content you have the legal right to watch.

Why isn't my playlist loading?

Double-check that the URL you pasted is correct and starts with http:// or https://. Ensure the link points directly to a valid M3U or M3U8 file and that your internet connection is active.

Can I add multiple playlists?

Yes. You can manage multiple playlist sources in the app and easily switch between them.

Is it free?

Yes. Liquid Glass Player is completely free and contains no ads, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

Is it private?

Yes — your playlists are processed locally on your device, and the app directly streams from the sources without going through any intermediate servers we control. See our Privacy Policy for details.
